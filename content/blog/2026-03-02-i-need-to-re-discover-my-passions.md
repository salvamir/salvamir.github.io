&nbsp; &nbsp;Lately I’ve been thinking about how many hobbies I stopped practicing because “nothing“. I just never did them again. Or at least not in the same way. I used to be the “music guy“ for everyone in my social circle and today I can’t say that. I used to write short stories a lot, and draw and go hiking on Saturday. I don’t want to say “college fault“ to everything I mentioned here, that isn’t true. Maybe I’m being just over distracted by my phone but I don’t that could kill all my real activities forever.

&nbsp; &nbsp;Today I’m starting the journey of supervising myself in which activities I do daily.&nbsp;
